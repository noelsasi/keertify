When to use each color
Ink #1A1208
Primary buttons, icon bg, bold text
Your dominant dark. Use for anything that needs the most weight and authority.
Gold #B8860B
Highlights, active state, logo accent
Sparingly — gold loses power when overused. Reserve for CTAs and key moments.
Crimson #7B1E2E
Tags, badges, featured labels
Secondary accent. Creates warmth and depth alongside gold.
Ivory #FAF7F0
Page bg, large surfaces
Warmer than white — gives the app a parchment / scripture feel.
